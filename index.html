Welcome to My Food Recipe Website

In this site you will find delicious meals to cook at home. These recipes are easy to follow and perfect for beginners who want to improve their cooking skills.

Recipe Steps

Cinnamon Chicken Preparation

Step 1: Marinate the chicken with cinnamon and spices.
Step 2: Let it rest for 10 minutes.
Step 3: Cook until fully done.

Sushi Preparation

Step 1: Prepare and season the rice.
Step 2: Add fillings like vegetables or fish.
Step 3: Roll and slice carefully.

Omelet Preparation

Step 1: Beat the eggs in a bowl.
Step 2: Heat the pan and add oil.
Step 3: Pour eggs and cook evenly.
